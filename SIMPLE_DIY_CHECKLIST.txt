TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------

   Problem: Can't connect from other computers
   Solution:
   - Check firewall is open (port 8000)
   - Ping the computer: ping COMPUTER-IP
   - Check chatbot is running on the computer

   Problem: Chatbot stops after reboot
   Solution:
   - Check Startup folder has the .bat file
   - Or set up Task Scheduler instead

   Problem: Computer goes to sleep
   Solution:
   - Settings ‚Üí Power ‚Üí Never sleep (both battery and plugged in)
   - Make sure power cable is plugged in!
